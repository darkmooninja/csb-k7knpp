About Us
The ballot box may seem intimidating, especially when someone has just turned 18 years old and is not familiar with complex policies and candidate voting histories. The We Vote Project is making sure that everyone can hit the ground running. Pre-registering to vote will ensure that everyone is ready to vote and the forum will allow students to critically think about policies.
